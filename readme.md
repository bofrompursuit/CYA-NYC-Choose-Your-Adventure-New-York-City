## 🌟 Overview

**CYA NYC** is an interactive, night-out curation tool designed to build custom nightlife and social adventures across New York City. From iconic jazz spots and hidden speakeasies to late-night dance floors, CYA NYC helps you decide your night on the fly.

> *"Powered by bad decisions and impeccable taste."*
